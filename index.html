In Fall 2024, I set out on a journey across the globe, asking the same question to everyone I met:

“Who is God to you?”

The responses I received were as diverse as the people themselves—ranging from Panamanian nature worshippers and Swiss atheists to Franciscan friars and Greek painters. Yet, despite the differences, there was something deeply universal in their answers.

Everywhere I went, I saw how humans are searching for a connection to the divine. This project is a visual representation of those responses and a reflection of my realization:

The globally shared prevalence of divine connection reveals how much more alike we are than different.
